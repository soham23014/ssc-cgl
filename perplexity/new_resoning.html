Priority Order

Tier A: Analogy, Classification, Series, Alphabet Series, Coding-Decoding, Missing Number Series.

These should be your fastest scoring areas. Build speed here first, then move to mixed practice.

Tier B: Blood Relation, Direction, Order Ranking.

These are important, but they become easy only when you use diagrams and formulas consistently.

Daily rule: 2 Tier A topics + 1 Tier B topic + 20 mixed questions.

Time rule: If a question does not show a pattern in 20 seconds, mark it and move on.

Revision rule: Revisit formulas, then PYQs, then timed tests.

Coding-Decoding Master Tricks

  • Try direct letter shift first.
  • Then check reverse alphabet using 27 - position.
  • Then convert letters to numbers with A=1 to Z=26.
  • Then test word order or rearrangement.
  • Finally, check symbol coding or mixed patterns.
