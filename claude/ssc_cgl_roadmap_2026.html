🎯 Smart PYQ strategy for a working person
1. Don't do full papers first — solve topic-wise PYQs by chapter (e.g., all Profit/Loss Qs from 2019–2024)
2. Kiran's Chapterwise PYQ book is your bible — 8000+ Qs sorted by topic
3. After Week 8, switch to full timed papers (shift-wise from Testbook/Oliveboard)
4. Maintain an error log — note wrong Qs + reason → review every Sunday
5. Focus on last 3 years most — pattern repeats heavily after 2021
